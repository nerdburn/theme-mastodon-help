Getting Started

Thanks for visiting the Mastodon Help Center. If you're new to Mastodon, we recommend you read the three common questions below:

Three Common Questions

How do I create or send an email?
Simply open up Evernote on your desktop or mobile, create a note in your Mastodon notebook, tag it send and also with your list send tag, and then sync Evernote. To learn more about sending emails, check out the Sending Emails section.

My emails aren't sending, what do I do?
There are a number of reasons an email might not send. Usually it's because the note is not tagged with both send and the lists send tag. For more details, check out the Sending Emails section.

I can't log in, how do I reset my password?
To reset your password, simply click the Trouble Logging In link on the log in page. It will ask you for your email address and send you a link to update your password.
